Guava Social

Guava Social is the answer to a problem most people feel but nobody's solved yet. We've built social platforms for followers, dating apps for swiping, professional networks for networking, but nothing designed around the three things that actually make a life feel full: love, friendship, and purpose.

Guava Social is a platform that uses AI to put you in the right room with the right person, intentionally, in the real world. Not to grow your following. Not to optimize your profile. To actually connect.

It starts with love, blind dating where AI matches you on compatibility, not appearances. But the vision is bigger than dating. It's a platform that knows you deeply enough to facilitate the connections that matter across all three dimensions of a meaningful life.
